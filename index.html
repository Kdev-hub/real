About Royal

Royal is a multi-faceted company with proven track record of successfully delivering a one-stop resource for all real estate needs. Established in 2024, with over 60 employees out of our Nigerian offices, our team leverages unparalleled expertise and income producing strategies to maximize the value of investment real estate. We are capable of executing real estate transactions of any size in Nigeria. Our property management services, powered by Poplar Homes, cover multi-family, office, retail and industrial assets. We specialize in luxury and garden-style apartment sales. Our investment brokerage team executes over $100M in sales annually. Our construction arm focuses on adaptive reuse and multifamily renovations. And we provide REO Management and Receivership services for lenders of distressed assets.
